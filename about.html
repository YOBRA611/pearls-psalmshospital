Who we are

Pearls & Psalms Hospital - Touching Lives Through Quality Healthcare

Pearls and Psalms Hospital is a new facility in the healthcare space of Western Kenya, however it is founded based on a long standing history of dedication, focus, commitment and the need to provide the best possible healthcare solutions. The foundation arises from our primary facility “Kaka Medical Centre” which has continued to provide quality health care to the adult and aging population of Kakamega county and Western Kenya at large.
Since our establishment in 2024, we have upheld a tradition of excellence in cost effective patient care and medical education, with the intention to delve into clinical research in the near future. Our hospital is staffed by a multidisciplinary team of highly trained doctors, clinical officers, nurses, and allied health professionals who are committed to provide the best available care on the background of evidence based innovation, and continuous improvement. With state-of-the-art facilities and advanced medical technology, we are equipped to provide specialized care equally committed to public health initiatives that promote wellness and disease prevention within the broader community.

Tips on Malaria

How to Prevent and Manage Malaria

Preventing malaria means avoiding mosquito bites and seeking treatment early.

  • • Sleep under treated mosquito nets.
  • • Clear stagnant water around your home.
  • • Wear long sleeves and use mosquito repellents.
  • • Visit a health facility if you have fever or chills.
  • • Take antimalarial medicine as prescribed.

Preventing malaria starts with protection, early diagnosis, and proper treatment to keep you and your family healthy.
