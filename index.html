Tips on Diabetes

How to Manage Diabetes Effectively

Managing diabetes means eating well, staying active, monitoring sugar, and caring for your mind.

  • • Eat veggies, whole grains, and lean proteins.
  • • Stay active walk, swim, or do light exercises daily.
  • • Check blood sugar and follow doctor’s advice.
  • • Manage stress with hobbies and relaxation.
  • • Limit sugar, processed foods, and avoid smoking.
  • • Stay hydrated and get enough sleep every night.

Managing diabetes means building healthy habits to control blood sugar and stay well.
